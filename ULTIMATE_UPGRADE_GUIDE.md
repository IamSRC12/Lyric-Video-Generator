## 🤖 AI Features Explained

### Emotion Detection:
The AI analyzes your lyrics for keywords and patterns:
- **Happy**: love, joy, smile → Gold/Yellow colors
- **Sad**: cry, tears, lonely → Blue colors
- **Energetic**: dance, jump, party → Magenta/Bright colors
- **Calm**: peace, quiet, soft → Sky Blue colors
- **Romantic**: love, heart, kiss → Pink colors
- **Angry**: hate, mad, rage → Red/Orange colors

### Rhythm Analysis:
- **Fast** (>140 BPM): Quick animations (0.3s)
- **Medium** (80-140 BPM): Normal speed (0.6s)
- **Slow** (<80 BPM): Slower animations (1.0s)
- **Syncopated**: Varied timing for dynamic feel

### Smart Suggestions:
AI considers:
1. Lyric content and theme
2. Detected emotion
3. Rhythm and tempo
4. Previous segment context
5. Overall song structure
